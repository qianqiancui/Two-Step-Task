Nice work! You may take a break.

When you start playing again, everything is the same as it was before this break.

  To choose the LEFT Advisor, press the F key.

  To choose the RIGHT Advisor, press the J key.

After you choose an Advisor, you will see the stock they invested in. When you see the stock, press the SPACEBAR to reveal the stock's performance and how many points you earned.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
