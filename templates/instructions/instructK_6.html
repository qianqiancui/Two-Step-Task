If your chosen Advisor's stock performs very well, you will earn a lot of points:

+9

However, sometimes an Advisor's stock doesn't perform well, and you only earn a few points:

+1

The quality of each stock will change during the game. A stock that was bad at the beginning of the game might start performing well, and a stock that initially pays well might perform poorly later on.

Fluctuations in a stock's performance will happen slowly. So, you have to focus to earn as many points as possible by hiring the Advisor with the best stock at each point in time.
