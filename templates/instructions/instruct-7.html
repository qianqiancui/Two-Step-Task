Instructions

In sum: You will play two phases of the game, both involving choices made by the Deciders about how much to share with the Recipient (you). As a reminder, the Deciders will not know the identity of the Recipient.

On each round, you will see two Deciders on the screen and pick one to interact with. You will then see the Decider’s point pool (“Out of:”) and how much the Decider shared (“Shared:”). You can make these choices using the [F] and [J] buttons on the keyboard.

To interact with the Decider on the LEFT side of the screen, press [F].

To interact with the Decider on the RIGHT side of the screen, press [J].

Click [NEXT] to continue.
